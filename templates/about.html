Anime Times

Welcome to Anime Times, your ultimate tool for keeping up with your favorite anime series! This web application allows you to create a personalized schedule to follow the weekly airing times of various anime. Additionally, you can search for anime titles and get detailed information about each show, including summaries, episode lists, and airing dates.

Key Features:

  • Weekly Schedule: Easily track the airing times of your favorite anime series throughout the week.
  • Anime Searcher: Find information on any anime with a simple search function
  • Detailed Anime Info: Access comprehensive details about each anime, including genre, release date, synopsis, and more.

Anime Times is designed to enhance your anime-watching experience by ensuring you never miss an episode. Whether you're a casual viewer or a dedicated otaku, this app is tailored to meet your needs.

Technologies Used

  • Flask

    A lightweight WSGI web application framework in Python that powers the backend of the app.

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScrtipt

    For building the structure of the web pages, applying styles to create an appealing layout, and adding interactivity and dynamic content

  • AniList GraphQL

    Used to fetch up-to-date information about various anime series.
